=> For phone, tablet devices (this tag makes your reponsive and mobile - friendly)
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0"/>


name="viewport" = the visible area of the web page on a device screen

width=device-width 
    this make the width of the page match the screen's width (like the width of the phone or tablet)

initial-scale=1.0
    initial zoom level when the page first loads.
    1.0 means no zoom - it shows the page at 100% scale
